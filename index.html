About me

Hello, I'm Daniela! I am a Physics PhD student at Rice University, working on CMS Heavy Ions. In my research, I work with large datasets, looking for charmonium produced in collisions at the Large Hadron Collider. At Rice, I serve as a Graduate Student Ambassador and as the table tennis club president!


Research experience:

    • Researcher @ Rice university

      Currently working with CMS on Ultraperipheral collisions (UPCs) on charmonium detection. Learn more about in Li lab.

    • Researcher @ Columbia University/Barnard College

      Working with Prof. Mukherjee on the data analysis of Blazars that are monitored by VERITAS. Learn more about my research here.

    • Researcher @ California Institute of Technology

      During the 2022 Summer, I spent 10 weeks working on a pipeline to analyse the sensitivity of the LIGO network. Here is my final report: LIGO.
